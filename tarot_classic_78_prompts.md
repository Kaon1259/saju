## ⚠️ 미드저니 차단 회피 가이드 (적용 완료)

정통 RWS 도상은 미드저니 콘텐츠 필터에 잘 걸립니다. 아래 표현은 의미 보존하며 안전하게 치환했습니다:

| 원래 표현 | 안전 표현 | 적용 카드 |
|---|---|---|
| `nude / naked` | `flowing classical drapery` / `white tunic` / `white shroud` | Lovers, Star, Sun, Judgement, World |
| `Baphomet / inverted pentagram / chained` | `mythic horned guardian` / `five-pointed star symbol` / `loosely tethered with golden cords` | Devil |
| `armored skeleton` | `silent rider in pale bone-white plate armor and shadowed helm` | Death |
| `pierced by swords / piercing back` | `framed by swords` / `planted upright around` | 3·10 of Swords |
| `severed head` | `veiled crowned visage` | 7 of Cups |
| `falling from burning windows` | `leaping from windows glowing with light` | Tower |
| `pope` | `venerable elder priest` | Hierophant |
| `bound and blindfolded` | `with white silk sash over eyes, encircled by` | 8 of Swords |
| `weeping / wounded / bandaged / mock battle / thief` | `sorrowful / weary / training match / stealthy figure` | 5·9 of Wands, 9 of Swords, 7 of Swords |

**여전히 차단되면**: `--v 6.1` → `--v 5.2` 또는 `--niji 6` 으로 시도, 또는 해당 단어 추가 완화(예: `young couple` → `two graceful figures`).
