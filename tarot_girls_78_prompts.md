## 공통 베이스 스타일 (모든 카드 앞면에 적용)

```
cartoon illustration, cute 20s Korean girl, soft pastel palette,
clean line art, anime-inspired, full-body or upper-body composition,
centered subject, ornate golden tarot card border frame with decorative
filigree corners, art nouveau ornamental edges, card title banner at
bottom in elegant serif, vintage mystical background, 2:3 portrait ratio,
high detail, trading card layout, no text artifacts
```
